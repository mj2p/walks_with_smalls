Hello, Thanks for visiting our Walks with Smalls site.

The idea is simple. Share your local knowledge, and walk more!

As parents to three small boys getting out is essential at the best of times, even more so now. Sometimes we find it hard to recall the beautiful local walks we've been on, or take a punt on a recommendation without a bit more information.

Hopefully this site can serve as a repository for sharing walks, and any useful tips to make them a pleasure not a chore.

This site is a lock-down project. It's not run for profit, just for fun.
